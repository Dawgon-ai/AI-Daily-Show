# UI SOP (ui_sop.md)

## Design System: "Ascribe"
- **Theme**: Modern Brutalist / High Energy.
- **Background**: `#000000` (Pure Black).
- **Primary/Accent**: `#BFF549` (Lime Green).
- **Typography**: 'Inter', sans-serif.
    - H1: 96px (Hero)
    - H2: 48px (Headings)
    - Body: 24px (Readability)
- **Shapes**: Sharp edges (Border Radius: `0px`).
- **Spacing**: Base unit `8px`.

## Colors
- **Background**: `#000000`
- **Surface**: `#111111` (Slightly lighter for cards, or just border)
- **Text Main**: `#FFFFFF` (Inferred for contrast against black)
- **Text on Accent**: `#000000`
- **Link/Muted**: `#99A1AF`
- **Border**: `#333333`

## Component Structure
1.  **Header**:
    - Minimalist. Logo + clean controls.
2.  **Grid**:
    - High contrast cards.
3.  **Article Card**:
    - 1px Border (Grey or Lime on hover).
    - No shadow (flat).
    - Sharp buttons.

## Interaction
- **Hover**: Shift accent color or invert colors.
- **Active**: `#BFF549` glow.
